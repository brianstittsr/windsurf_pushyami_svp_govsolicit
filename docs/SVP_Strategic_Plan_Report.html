10. Risks & Mitigation (Continued)

Detailed mitigation plans for critical risks

Critical Risk: Economic Downturn

Probability: Medium | Impact: High

Mitigation:

  • Build recurring revenue to 25% of total
  • Develop "efficiency" positioning for downturns
  • Maintain 6-month cash reserve
  • Create flexible pricing models
  • Diversify across industries

Critical Risk: Key Affiliate Departure

Probability: Medium | Impact: High

Mitigation:

  • Maintain 2:1 affiliate-to-project ratio
  • Document all processes and client knowledge
  • Implement non-compete agreements
  • Build strong affiliate community
  • Create career progression paths

High Risk: OEM Requirement Changes

Probability: High | Impact: Medium

Mitigation:

  • Maintain OEM supplier development relationships
  • Participate in industry standards organizations
  • Build modular, adaptable service offerings
  • Create early warning system
